Q: How to replace a lost ID
A: Office involved: Class Adviser; Admin Office; School Cashier; School Head. Steps: 1) Report the loss to your Class Adviser immediately. 2) Get and fill out the ID Replacement Request Form. 3) Class Adviser endorses to Admin for record verification. 4) Request is forwarded to the School Head for approval. 5) After approval, Admin or Class Adviser issues a new ID or temporary gate pass. Reference: DepEd Order No. 41, s. 2012; School-based policy.

Q: How to replace a lost report card
A: Office involved: Class Adviser; Registrar; School Head. Steps: 1) Submit a written request to your Class Adviser. 2) Adviser evaluates and endorses to the Registrar. 3) Registrar verifies your academic records. 4) Request goes to the School Head for approval. 5) Registrar releases a certified true copy of the report card. Reference: DepEd Order No. 8, s. 2015; DepEd Order No. 41, s. 2012.

Q: How to enroll
A: Office involved: Enrollment Committee; Registrar; Guidance Office. Steps: 1) Get an enrollment form from the Registrar. 2) Submit the accomplished form plus requirements (SF9, Birth Certificate, JHS Certificate of Completion, etc.). 3) Registrar evaluates and validates the records. 4) Guidance Office confirms strand placement if applicable. 5) You are officially enrolled after confirmation and encoding. Reference: DepEd Order No. 3, s. 2018; Annual DepEd Enrollment Guidelines Memorandum.

Q: When will requested records or documents be released? How long does it take to process documents?
A: Office involved: Registrar. Steps: 1) Request is logged and acknowledged by the Registrar. 2) Records are verified and prepared. 3) Approved documents are released within the processing period. Q: How much is the uniform? Uniform cost
A: Office involved: School Administration; Authorized School Supplier. Q: School Background, History
A: Senior High School within Bacoor Elementary School was established in 2016. It is one of the five public senior high schools in the Division of Bacoor City. It started catering to students under the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Strand, and later added the Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand in 2020. SHS within Bacoor ES was first housed in SHS Dulong Bayan, alongside SHS Dulong Bayan and SHS within Sineguelasan. In June 2021, the school was transferred and housed within Bacoor ES. It was originally named SHS Alima, as Bacoor ES is situated in Barangay Alima, but was later renamed to SHS within Bacoor Elementary School. The school's main gate is situated in Barangay Campo Santo, making it a proud constituent of the local community.
